What is DBT Seeding?

DBT seeding is the process of enabling your bank account to receive Direct Benefit Transfer payments from government schemes, including scholarships, using Aadhaar authentication.

üîó

DBT-Enabled Account

A DBT-enabled account is specifically configured to receive government benefits and scholarships directly, ensuring faster and secure fund transfers.

‚úÖ

Benefits

Ensures faster, transparent, and secure transfer of scholarships directly to your account without any intermediaries or delays.

DBT Seeding vs Aadhaar Linking: Know the Difference

üîó Aadhaar Linking

Basic
  • Simple connection of Aadhaar to bank account
  • Used for identification purposes
  • May not support DBT transactions
  • Basic KYC compliance
  • Limited to account verification
Note: This alone is NOT sufficient for scholarship payments

üå± DBT Seeding

Recommended
  • Complete DBT enablement of your account
  • Enables government benefit transfers
  • Supports scholarship payments
  • Full compliance with DBT requirements
  • Direct fund transfer capability
Required: Essential for receiving scholarship payments

Important: For scholarship payments, you need DBT-enabled Aadhaar seeding, not just basic Aadhaar linking!
